About BluePeak HVAC AI

BluePeak HVAC AI provides guided diagnostics and structured reporting for HVAC service teams. We focus on practical workflows that help technicians capture consistent data and help managers review cases with clarity.

Mission

Support service organizations with dependable diagnostic guidance and clear reporting so teams can make informed decisions and document work efficiently.

Who we serve

BluePeak is designed for licensed HVAC technicians, service managers, and operational leaders who need consistent diagnostics, documentation, and handoff between field and office teams.

What we build

A guided workflow for capturing equipment details and measurements, paired with structured outputs that surface findings, recommendations, and next steps. BluePeak is designed to support professional judgment, not replace it.
