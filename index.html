What you get

Structured diagnostic output

Observations, recommended tests, and findings grouped by system, not raw AI text.

Confidence and limitations

Every diagnosis includes a confidence level and explains what data is still needed.

OEM-aware guidance

Matched models use OEM ranges when available. Typical ranges are flagged when OEM data is not found.

PDF and email-ready reports

Clean summaries for internal handoff, service records, or customer communication.

How it works

Create a case

Select job type, add notes, and define the fault context.

Enter equipment details and readings

Nameplates and field measurements are captured manually or via OCR (plan-dependent).

Generate analysis and reports

The system evaluates inputs, explains confidence, and recommends next tests.

What we need for reliable results

To reach higher diagnostic confidence, senior technicians typically check:

Return and supply temperatures

Or delta-T and ambient temps.

Pressures, superheat, subcooling

As applicable to the system.

Fault codes

System alerts and diagnostics.

Airflow or static pressure

Recommended for full confidence.

Missing inputs reduce confidence. This is how experienced technicians decide what to check next.
