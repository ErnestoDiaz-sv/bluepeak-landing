What we need for reliable results

  • Return/supply temps (or delta T) and ambient temps.
  • Pressures, superheat/subcooling (as applicable).
  • Fault codes.
  • Airflow/static pressure (recommended).

Missing critical inputs reduces confidence and prevents firm conclusions.
